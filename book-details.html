The Tunnel

Author: Ruskin bond

Price: $70.00

The steam engine shot out of the tunnel, snorting and puffing like a green, black and gold dragon, a beautiful monster out of Ranji's dreams...

Reviews

Michael Brown: Rating: ★★★★☆
"Reading 'The Tunnel' by Ruskin Bond is like taking a serene walk through the Indian hills. The story is beautifully written, with Bond's signature style of highlighting the simplicity and beauty of rural life. Ranji's curiosity and his bond with the tunnel watchman is depicted with a gentle touch that only Bond can master. The story is short, but it encapsulates a range of emotions and leaves a lasting impression. Bond's detailed descriptions and empathetic portrayal of his characters make this a delightful read. It's a perfect book to relax with on a quiet afternoon."

Merry: Rating: ★★★★★
"Ruskin Bond's 'The Tunnel' is a masterful piece of storytelling. His ability to capture the essence of rural India and the innocence of childhood is unparalleled. The narrative is rich with vivid imagery, and Bond's prose flows effortlessly, making it easy to get lost in the world he creates.The relationship between Ranji and the tunnel watchman is heartwarming and adds a layer of depth to the story. This book is a testament to Bond's talent in finding extraordinary tales in the most ordinary of settings.
